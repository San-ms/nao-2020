ABOUT

Hi again! my name is Sandra Méndez Sarmiento. After I graduated in 2011 as a Communications and Product Designer and before moving to Berlin (ja, ich kann gut deutsch!), I worked for six years as a Graphic Designer, Art Director and Visual Editor as a freelance and for various award winning design studios and independent publishing houses developing mainly, but not exclusively, branding and editorial projects. In January 2020 I got my MA Visual Strategies and Stories degree at the Burg Giebichenstein Kunsthoschule Halle.

In my projects I constantly question how design and visual culture perpetuate oppressive values and stereotypes, and propose ways to transform them through language and its materiality.

I'm currently available for either working on individual projects or becoming a permanent member of a team.

Looking forward to hear from you! Feel free to contact me via e-mail or let's share some stories here.
