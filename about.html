ABOUT

Hola! I'm San a communication designer specializing in visual strategies and editorial design, currently learning web development (you're navigating my first web site). After I graduated in 2011 as a Communications and Product Designer and before moving to Berlin, I worked for six years as a Graphic Designer and Art Director for three award winning Design Studios, an independent publishing house and as freelance between Bogotá and Medellín mainly but not exclusively, developing Branding and Editorial projects (see more here). In January 2020 I got my MA Visual Strategies and Stories degree at the Burg Giebichenstein Kunsthoschule Halle.

The selected works you will find in this website represent my most personal and professional interests, where design meets activism in a powerful and active way, and the kind of projects I want to develope. I truly believe in the power of words and images, and I’ve decided to use them the best way I can to support little and big actions aiming to transform the mess we’ve done.

I’m currently available for either working on individual projects or becoming a permanent member of a team.

Looking forward to hear from you! Feel free to contact me via e-mail or let's share some stories here.
